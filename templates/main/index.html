About Us

KRIDASASHTRA

Kridasashtra is a pioneering sports science company dedicated to identifying and nurturing young athletic talent for international sports. Collaborating with India's premium sports leagues, we provide advanced assessments for school-going athletes, leveraging cutting-edge sports science methodologies. Our expertise lies in delivering state-of-the-art sports science equipment and solutions that enhance performance, recovery, and overall athletic development. With a mission to revolutionize sports training and talent identification, Kridasashtra bridges the gap between raw potential and professional excellence, ensuring that young athletes receive world-class guidance to compete at the highest levels.

Kridasashtra pioneers sports science in India, helping identify and train young athletes for global competition. We offer expert assessments, training tools, and recovery solutions to elevate athletic performance from an early age.
